Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Data Collection

LOGOS does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app operates entirely on-device with no server-side component.

Local Storage

The app uses your device's local storage to save your preferences (theme, font, text size, layout mode). This data never leaves your device. Clearing app data or reinstalling will reset these preferences to their defaults.

Internet & Permissions

LOGOS requires no internet connection after initial installation — the entire Bible text is bundled with the app. No network requests are made during normal use.

Third-Party Services

LOGOS does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

LOGOS does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated date above will reflect it. No material change will involve data collection — the app simply does not collect data.
